The superconducting transition temperature T_c (K) as a function of applied magnetic field. The calculation assumes a relationship obtained from inverting: Hc2(T) / Hc2(0) = [1 - (T / Tc)^2] / [1 + (T / Tc)^2]. See e.g.,: M. Tinkham, Phys. Rev. 129, 2413 (1963). https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRev.129.2413
